---- Summary ----

     A Script that eliminates the impact of the "Shortcut Virus"

---- Command syntax ----

     app devirus [Write your drive letter here]

---- Example ----

     app devirus C:

---- Description ----

     When your USB device get infected with "Shortcut Virus" that hides 
     all of your files in a folder without name you can use this Script 
     to get your data back on the root of your USB device
